About WelTec

Wellington Institute of Technology, also known as WelTec, is a leading provider of technical and professional education in New Zealand. Established in 1904, WelTec has a long history of delivering high-quality education and training that meets the needs of industry and the community. With over 6,000 students annually, WelTec offers a range of qualifications from certificates to degrees in various fields such as engineering, information technology, business, and creative industries. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our Category One status, the highest rating from the New Zealand Qualifications Authority (NZQA). WelTec provides students with the practical skills and knowledge required to succeed in today’s competitive job market.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to empower individuals through education and prepare them for successful careers.


Campuses

WelTec operates across several campuses in the Wellington region:

  • WelTec Petone Campus – The main campus, located in the heart of Petone.
  • WelTec Wellington CBD Campus – Focuses on business, IT, and creative industries.
  • WelTec Porirua Campus – Home to the School of Health and Social Services.
