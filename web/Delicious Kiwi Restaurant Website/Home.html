Welcome to Delicious Kiwi Restaurant

Our journey began over a decade ago when our founders, a team of passionate food enthusiasts, set out on a mission to create a dining experience that highlights the rich culinary heritage of New Zealand. From the lush pastures to the bountiful sea, our menu showcases the best of what this beautiful country has to offer.


At Delicious Kiwi, we believe that great food starts with great ingredients. That's why we take pride in sourcing our produce from local farmers, fishermen, and artisans who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Our skilled chefs craft each dish with precision, elevating traditional recipes with modern techniques and innovative twists.


Our restaurant is not just a place to eat; it's a place to connect, celebrate, and savour the joys of life. Whether you're enjoying a relaxed meal with family, hosting a special event, or simply grabbing a quick bite, our warm and welcoming ambiance sets the stage for a memorable dining experience.
